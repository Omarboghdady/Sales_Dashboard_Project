📊 Retail Sales Analysis - Excel Dashboard

Project Overview
This project analyzes sales data for a retail company operating across 4 branches in Egypt: Alexandria, Cairo, Giza, and Mansoura. The goal was to understand overall business performance, identify problem areas, and provide data-driven recommendations to support better decision-making.
Tools & Technologies
Microsoft Excel
Power Query
Pivot Tables
Excel Dashboard
Project Workflow
Step 1 — Power Query (Data Understanding & Cleaning)
The first step was exploring the raw dataset to understand its structure. I identified and removed duplicates and null values, standardized column names and data types, and ensured data consistency across all branches before moving to analysis.
Step 2 — Pivot Tables (Data Analysis)
Using Pivot Tables, I summarized sales, costs, and profits by branch and category. I analyzed return rates by city and product category, compared discount values across branches, extracted monthly sales trends throughout the year, and measured customer feedback scores by product category.
Step 3 — Dashboard Building (Data Visualization)
The final step was building 2 interactive dashboards in Excel to present the findings in a clear and visual way. Dashboard 1 covers the business overview, and Dashboard 2 focuses on problem detection.

Cairo has the highest return rate and the highest profit at the same time. This means that despite the high returns, Cairo is still the most profitable branch. However, if the excessive discounts were reduced, the profit margins could increase significantly.

Key Findings
Sales performance is strong across all branches
Cairo is responsible for 30% of all returns
Footwear accounts for 60% of Cairo's returns
Cairo's discounts are disproportionately high compared to other branches
Footwear has the most negative customer feedback across all categories
Recommendations
Review discount policy in Cairo — discounts are too high and eating into profit margins
Investigate Footwear quality — high returns and negative reviews suggest a product quality issue
Analyze Cairo's return reasons — understand why Cairo behaves differently from other branches
Improve product descriptions for Footwear — customer expectations may not match the actual product
